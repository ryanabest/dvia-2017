Mapping Space

This project reworks my prior visualization of the USGS earthquake feed to add a geospatial component.

Here, we first look only at magnitude, then connect earthquakes of the same magnitude with their location across the globe. The chart on the left plots the distribution of all M1.0+ earthquakes that occured within a month of 12.21.17 by magnitude. Hover over a point on this chart to see the specific magnitude of the event(s) below your cursor, along with the total corresponding number of events matching that magnitude. Click a point to see all these events on the map to the right, then hover over a highlighted point on the map to discover detail on the time and place of this event.
